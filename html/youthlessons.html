Youth Lessons Overview

Designed for ages 3–13 years, our Youth Swim Lessons offer a structured and supportive environment for swimmers of all skill levels. Whether your child is just learning to feel comfortable in the water or ready to refine stroke technique, our curriculum encourages steady growth using progressive skill-based levels.

  • Four-Week Sessions

  • One 30-minute group swim lesson each week

  • Maximum 4 students to 1 instructor

  • Session Pricing​

  • Multi-enrollment discounts

  • Weekday lesson times starting at 5:25 pm

  • Weekend lesson times starting at 8:00 am

How to Sign Up

Booking your spot is easy Just follow these steps:

  1. Create an account with us through the Customer Portal

  2. Add your student to the account

  3. Check to see when registration will open for the upcoming session

  4. When registration opens, sign your student up for the class you would like to take (sign up through the Customer Portal)

Financial assistance is available for all living in the 98052 zip code (Redmond) please click on this link  to fill out the form and send it to  contact@waveaquatics.org once you have completed filling it out.

Explore Levels & Class Descriptions

Our youth lessons follow a progressive, layered curriculum: Bubbles → Ripples → Tides → Swells → Rogues → Jaws → Mavericks. Each level builds on the previous one with skills tailored to competence and age.
