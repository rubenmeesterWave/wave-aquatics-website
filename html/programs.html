Programs Overview

Wave Aquatics offers dedicated lap swim and family swim sessions for swimmers of all ages and abilities. Whether you're training for fitness or just looking for some relaxing time in the water, our pool is here for you.

Youth Lessons

Our Youth Lessons provide a fun and safe environment for children ages 3-13. Classes focus on water safety, basic swim skills, and stroke development, with small instructor-to-student ratios for personalized attention.

Schedule: Mon & Wed, 5-6 PM

Session Length: 4 weeks

Max Students per Instructor: 4

Pricing:$120 per session

How to Register: Sign up through Customer Portal

 Learn More

Lap Swim

Lap Swim is open to swimmers of all levels aged 12 and up. Enjoy structured lanes for different speeds, a calm swimming environment, and the chance to improve endurance and technique.

Schedule: Daily, 6-8 AM & 5-7 PM

Lane Designation: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced

Pricing: $10 per session or monthly pass available

Requirements: Ages 12+, ages 12-17 must pass swim test

 Learn More
