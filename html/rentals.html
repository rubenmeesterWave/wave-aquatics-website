Pool Rentals

Teaching private lessons? Throwing a pool party? We have a space for you! Celebrate your next event at the pool! Private lessons, parties, club gatherings, events or just fun with friends.

  • Enjoy lap swimming in a private or shared lane setting.

  • Reserve a pool party space for friends, family, or club events.

  • Flexible rental times to fit your schedule and needs.

  • All safety guidelines must be followed during your rental.

Set up your payment account in our customer portal here:

Customer Portal

Contact our rental director for information on how to get set up

(425) 439-9207 (Call or Text!)

Or email us: Rentals@waveaquatics.org

Liability insurance for private instructors:

  • Contact the Rental Director to discuss your event or lesson needs and confirm availability for your preferred date and time.

  • Complete and submit any required forms, including waivers or rental agreements, through our Customer Portal or at the front desk.

  • Reserve your pool space online or by phone, specifying the number of lanes, rental type, and any additional equipment or services you need.

  • Make payment via the Customer Portal

  • Arrive on the day of your rental and check in at the front desk. The facility staff will guide you to your reserved area and provide any necessary instructions for a safe and enjoyable visit.
