Program Overview

Wave Aquatics offers dedicated lap swim and family swim sessions for swimmers of all ages and abilities. Whether you're training for fitness or just looking for some relaxing time in the water, our pool is here for you.

  • Lap swim is open to all swimmers aged 12 and up (ages 12-17 must pass a swim test).

  • Circle swimming is required when sharing lanes with others.

  • Please follow posted signs for lane speeds (slow, medium, fast).

  • Lap swim availability may vary by day—check the schedule for current times.​

How to Sign Up

Follow these steps:

  1. Create an account with us through the Customer Portal

  2. Add yourself or your swimmer as a student on your account

  3. Browse the “Lap Swim” sessions in the booking menu

  4. Register for the time you want

See Pool Rules

  • Drop-in available with punch passes or single visit payment.

  • Reserve your space online before you come in and check in at the front desk at arrival

  • Purchase punch passes via our Customer Portal.
