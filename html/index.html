Welcome to Wave Aquatics

Welcome to Wave Aquatics! Since 1986, we have provided an array of aquatics programs to Eastside community members of the greater Seattle area. Each year, nearly 2,000 young people and adults participate in our competitive swim teams for ages 5 and up, public and private swim lessons, the Lake Washington Masters swim team, pool rentals, and of course public swim every day.
