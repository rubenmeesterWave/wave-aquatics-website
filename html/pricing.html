Passes & Pricing

Wave Aquatics offers a variety of single swim passes, punch passes, and family passes. Use this page to see pricing and learn how to reserve swims.

Customer Portal

    Single Day Passes

    Day Passes are for individual enrollments only and apply to the specific swim session you register for.

  • Deep End Lap Swim: $10

  • Shallow End Lap Swim: $7.50

  • Family Swim: $7.50

  • Senior Swim (50+): $5

  • Shallow Water Aerobics: $5

  • Deep Water Aerobics: $10

  • Senior Deep Water Aerobics: $7.50

    Punch Passes

    Punch Passes can be purchased on the Point of Sale page in the Customer Portal.

  • Lap Swim/Family Swim 10 Punch Pass (STUDENT): $60

  • Lap Swim/Family Swim 10 Punch Pass (FAMILY): $60

  • Senior Swim/Shallow Aerobics 10 Punch Pass: $45

  • Deep Water Aerobics 10 Punch Pass: $80

  • Senior Deep Water Aerobics 10 Punch Pass: $65

  • To pay the $5 Senior rate for regular Lap Swim, apply the promo code "SENIOR" in the cart before checking out.

  • To pay the $5 Senior rate for Lap Swim—Shallow end only, apply the promo code "SENIOR SHALLOW" in the cart before checking out.

  • Reserve swims using your punch pass under My Account > Students > Passes > Use Punch Pass.

Family Punch Passes

What is a Family Punch Pass?
A Family Punch Pass is a 10 Punch Pass that can be used by any student within your family account.

Difference between Family and Student Punch Passes:
A Student Punch Pass is assigned to one student only. A Family Punch Pass can be used by any student in your family account.

How to Buy:
Start in the Customer Portal > Point of Sale > Passes to purchase the passes you need.

How to Use:
Reserve swims under My Account > Student > Passes > Use Punch Pass.
